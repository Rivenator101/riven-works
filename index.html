tech projects

Things I've built.

01
SoulScape
A digital safe space that turns emotions into living abstract art. Type how you feel — messy, uncertain, contradictory — and SoulScape translates it into a responsive visual environment. No scores, no labels, no judgment. Built for people tired of rating their feelings on a number scale. Honorable Mention — PeerBridge Mental Health Hacks 2025.

02
CalmCommute
Navigation that prioritises mental health over speed. Colour-coded stress routing, an "I need a break" button that auto-reroutes without asking, and built-in 4-7-8 breathing exercises. Built because I actually needed it. Google Maps Platform, React, Node.js.

The Goal
A wearable levitation system that works over any surface — concrete, wood, soil, water. No special floor needed. The device brings its own lift. Working toward this since age nine.

EAD Thrust
Electroaerodynamic propulsion — ionic wind from a high-voltage field, no moving parts. Pushes against air, not ground. Proven at aircraft scale by MIT in 2018.

Flux Pinning
Superconducting quantum flux pinning for passive three-axis stability. Zero ongoing energy cost. The only known levitation mechanism that is inherently stable.

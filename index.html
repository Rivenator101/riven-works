who i am

Builder. Researcher.
Fifteen years old.

I'm Riven — a fifteen-year-old independent researcher based in Ontario, Canada. I became obsessed with levitation when I was nine and held two magnets together for the first time. Most people let go. I didn't.

Six years later I'm building the theoretical and experimental foundation for a surface-agnostic personal levitation system — a wearable device that lifts a person off any surface, regardless of what it's made of. No special floors. No magnetic tracks. Anywhere.

I work independently, document everything, and I'm actively seeking academic mentorship from researchers in electroaerodynamics and superconductivity. I want to do this properly — with real math, real hardware, and real science.

The Goal
A wearable levitation system that works over any surface — concrete, wood, soil, water. No special floor needed. The device brings its own lift.
surface-agnostic
🌬️
EAD Thrust
Electroaerodynamic propulsion — ionic wind generated by a high-voltage field. No moving parts. Pushes against air, not ground. Proven at scale by MIT (2018).
mechanism A
🔮
Flux Pinning
Superconducting quantum flux pinning for passive three-axis stability. Zero ongoing energy cost. The only levitation mechanism that is inherently stable.
